Prototype coverage: EU ETS, UK ETS, Swiss ETS, CORSIA. Country boundaries are simplified placeholder shapes for this early version, not precise geography. Regulation text drawn from the same sourcing as the Climate Policies reference on the homepage. Carbon prices are indicative spot rates (EU ETS ~€65/t, UK ETS ~£45/t, CORSIA ~$5/t). Fuel burn figures are approximate cruise-phase values derived from published ICAO BFFM2 methodology and manufacturer data — actual costs will vary by load factor, altitude, and route profile. This does not constitute compliance advice - for filing or penalty decisions specific to a route or operator, consult a qualified compliance advisor.

Aviation Emissions Policy Map

Hover a country to see which emissions regimes apply. Open the route checker to search a departure and arrival airport (worldwide) and an aircraft type to see which regulations apply, the great-circle route on the map, and the estimated carbon cost. Coverage is currently limited to EU ETS, UK ETS, Swiss ETS and CORSIA - this is an early prototype, not a complete or authoritative regulatory reference.

Prototype coverage: EU ETS, UK ETS, Swiss ETS, CORSIA. Country boundaries are simplified placeholder shapes for this early version, not precise geography. Regulation text drawn from the same sourcing as the Climate Policies reference on the homepage. Airport locations from the OpenFlights airport database (~6,000 airports with scheduled/commercial service). Route distance is the great-circle (shortest-path) distance between the two airports; actual flown distance will be longer due to routing, airspace, and weather. Carbon prices are indicative spot rates (EU ETS ~€65/t, UK ETS ~£45/t, CORSIA ~$5/t). Fuel burn figures are approximate cruise-phase values derived from published ICAO BFFM2 methodology and manufacturer data — actual costs will vary by load factor, altitude, and route profile. This does not constitute compliance advice - for filing or penalty decisions specific to a route or operator, consult a qualified compliance advisor.

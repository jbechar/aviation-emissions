Aviation Emissions // Explained

A simplified visual breakdown of what actually drives aviation's climate impact:
contrail cirrus, CO2, and net NOx — shown as a share of aviation's radiative forcing (RF).

Live site
https://jbechar.github.io/aviation-emissions/

Source
RF central estimates: Lee et al. (2021), *Atmospheric Environment*.
Contrail concentration finding: Teoh et al. (2020, 2024).

Non-CO2 multiplier: why we show a range, not a number

Evidence for model uncertainty. Not used to derive any figure on this page.

Fleet share and CO2 exposure are scenario ceilings, not measured outcomes. NOx is certification-grade at climb-out; cruise correction via the Boeing Fuel Flow Method is the documented next step. The multiplier range exists to show why non-CO2 effects aren't collapsed into a single figure here, not to produce one.

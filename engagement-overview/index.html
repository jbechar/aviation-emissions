Engagement Overview

Emissions & Compliance Literacy for Aviation Teams

Plain-language education on what EU ETS, UK ETS, Swiss ETS, CORSIA and CSRD actually require, plus ongoing tracking of how those requirements are changing and what applies to a specific fleet or operation.

What this is

Two things, delivered together: education that makes emissions policy and mechanisms genuinely understandable to non-specialist teams, and ongoing tracking that flags what's changed in the regulatory landscape and what it means for a specific fleet. This is education and monitoring, not compliance strategy or legal advice - for filing decisions or penalty exposure specific to your operation, that sits with your qualified compliance advisor or legal counsel. What's provided here is the clarity that makes those conversations faster and better-informed.

Where this comes from

Built on real technical grounding, not just summary
Includes original, certified-data analysis of aviation mechanical and credit events - most recently the GTF grounding crisis and the SMBC/JSA fleet exposure case (44 aircraft, ~$173.5M annual re-placement value) - alongside an interactive, uncertainty-aware breakdown of radiative forcing by pollutant.

Typical scope per engagement

EducationPlain-language briefings or training materials on ETS/CORSIA/CSRD mechanisms, thresholds, and cost drivers, tailored to the team's existing familiarity
TrackingRegular updates on regulatory changes across EU ETS, UK ETS, Swiss ETS, CORSIA and CSRD, with what changed, when, and what it means for a specific fleet - sourced and dated, not interpreted as legal advice
OutputWritten briefings, one-page updates, or a live-tracked reference page, depending on what fits the team's workflow
CadenceOne-off briefing, or ongoing tracking on a regular cycle (monthly/quarterly), depending on scope agreed

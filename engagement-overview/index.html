Engagement Overview

Fleet Exposure Analysis - Scope & Engagement Terms

Bespoke, quantified fleet financial exposure and emissions analysis, available as a direct engagement or as a subcontracted specialist under advisory firms, lessors, or consultancies.

What this is

Quantified fleet financial exposure and emissions analysis, tied to specific aviation mechanical or credit events - engine groundings, lessor lease rejections, fleet retirements. Each engagement produces a written brief with certified or clearly-labelled source data, financial exposure figures, and emissions/regulatory context where relevant.

Example deliverable

SMBC / Jackson Square Aviation exposure brief
44 rejected aircraft following Spirit Aviation Holdings' Chapter 11, ~$173.5M annual re-placement value, 194,000-243,000 tonnes CO2 exposure range depending on replacement scenario.

Typical scope per engagement

Data sourcingCertified engine/emissions data (ICAO EEDB), fleet and lease records, published financial filings, court/bankruptcy filings where relevant
AnalysisFinancial exposure modelling (re-placement value, lease drag), emissions exposure ranges, scenario analysis where assumptions are uncertain
OutputWritten brief (PDF/web), optional live interactive tool, all figures labelled by source and confidence level
TimelineTypically 1-3 weeks depending on data availability and scope

Working basis

Available for direct engagements and as a subcontracted specialist under advisory firms, lessors, or consultancies needing this analysis for their own clients. Standard confidentiality terms apply; happy to sign an NDA before any client-specific detail is shared. All figures clearly labelled as certified, modelled, or scenario-based - no blending of confirmed and estimated data.
